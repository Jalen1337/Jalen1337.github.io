Happy Birthday Dad

December 3, 1961

On your special day, I just want to take a moment to tell you how much you mean to me. You’ve been my rock, my guide, and my greatest supporter. Your wisdom, love, and strength have shaped me into the person I am today, and I am forever grateful for everything you’ve done for me.

I hope today brings you as much joy and happiness as you’ve brought into my life. May this year be filled with new adventures, good health, and countless moments of joy. Thank you for being the amazing father you are.
